The <info>%command.name%</info> command generates or updates your config.yml,
services.yml, routing.yml and composer.json to kickstart local application
development within your Contao Managed Edition project.

<fg=yellow>Note:</> you can ignore the <fg=yellow>It looks like your app may be using a namespace 
other than 'App'</> notice. This notice will occur when you have not yet added
an autoload directive to your composer.json for your app namespace (which
the command will do for you).
